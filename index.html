DUA LIPA

Dua Lipa is a British singer-songwriter known for her powerful voice and catchy pop hits. She gained widespread fame with songs like "New Rules," "Don't Start Now," and "Levitating." Her music often blends pop, disco, and R&B influences, and she's recognized for her distinctive style and empowering lyrics. Besides her music, she's also an advocate for various social issues and has won numerous awards, including Grammys.
